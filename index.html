Apple Pie

This is a secret recipe passed over generations by my ancestor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp groud cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instruction

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, cinnamon, and apples.
  2. Beat the dry ingredients together until smooth.
  3. Pour the smooth mixture into a greased 9-inch pie dish.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Cool the pie on the counter for at least 1 hour before serving.
